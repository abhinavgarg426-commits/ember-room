Our Story

A Passion for Flavor

The Ember Room was born from Chef Arjun Kapoor's love for bringing people together through food. After training at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and working at Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe, he returned to Mumbai with a vision — to create a dining experience that honors Indian ingredients and techniques while embracing global inspiration.

Every dish on our menu tells a story of tradition meeting innovation — smoky tandoor flavors paired with French sauces, coastal spices elevated with Japanese precision.

"Food is the memory that stays long after the evening ends."

— Chef Arjun Kapoor, Founder
