Our Story

Started in My Mother's
Kitchen in 2003

The Coconut Leaf began with my mother Meenakshi running a tiny 8-table mess in Nungambakkam. She made dosas on a iron griddle, ground her masalas by hand every morning at 5 AM, and refused to compromise on coconut oil even when it was expensive.

22 years later, nothing has changed except the size. Same family recipes, same dedication to real food. My wife and I run it now, and our kids help on weekends. This is our family's contribution to keeping South Indian vegetarian food honest.

"If my grandmother wouldn't recognize the taste, we don't serve it."

— Murali Krishnan, Founder
